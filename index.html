Bio

My name is Ashley Faltis, I am 18 years old and i have been very into digital art for about 3 years now. I started my journey self-taught and in 2019 i started attending an art school, that is when my art skills really started to develop. I focus on Portrait drawings but have recently started drawing landscapes. I would love to take my art a step further and aim to become a games designer and animator. I take commissions and always love trying new things.
